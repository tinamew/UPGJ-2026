**Ligayang Litrato**

Ligayang Litrato is a narrative driven puzzle game where Rene quits her soul-sucking corporate job and moves back to the province to take over her family's old business
of restoring photos and tapping into the hearts of her customers. 

Build Instructions:
Unzip the zip and run the .exe file, and enjoy!
